Mobile Audio Fix

Problem: You hear background music but not dialogue.
Solution: This is because mobile browsers can't decode 5.1 surround sound. Use transcoded audio streams that convert to stereo.

Mobile Streaming Tips

  • Transcoded Audio: Best for mobile
  • Mobile Optimized: 2-channel stereo
  • Avoid Direct Stream: Often has 5.1 audio
  • VLC App: Best overall solution

Technical Details

  • Movies often have 5.1 audio tracks
  • Mobile browsers only support stereo
  • Background music is in all channels
  • Dialogue is often only in center channel
  • Transcoding mixes channels to stereo
